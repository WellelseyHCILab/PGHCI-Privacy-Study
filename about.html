About

The HCIPGP research project aims to improve the design of websites and tools that make personal genomic information more accessible and understandable by the general public.

We seek to learn more about how users, who are non-experts in genetics, can engage and learn from personal genomic information.

In this study, we will ask you to:

  1. Answer a few questions about how you use the Internet;
  2. Read about personal genomics and take a brief quiz about the information;
  3. Explore a personal genomic report of a user named Jamie and answer questions about the report;
  4. Explore a consent form for a hypothetical study involving a genomic report and answer questions about the consent form;
  5. Answer some demographic questions;

The time required for participating in this study is about 1 hour and your responses will be kept anonymous.
